        <h2>NOTES ON SYLLABLES AND STRESS</h2>
        <div >
            <ul><li>A hyphen ( - ) separates the syllables.</li></ul>
            <ul><li>A syllable printed in <b>BOLD CAPITAL LETTERS</b> has the primary stress.</li></ul>
            <ul><li>A syllable in CAPITAL LETTERS has the secondary stress.</li></ul>
            <ul><li>All other syllables are in lower case letters.</li></ul>
            <ul><li>Unstressed vowels are always in lower case letters.</li></ul>
        </div>
        <div style="margin-left:40px;">
            Example: AY-dree-<b>AH</b>-nuh
        </div>
